Our Events

Our Plan

The UC Civil and Environmental Society is dedicated to bridging the gap between academic study and professional practice. Our events are intentionally designed to foster a tight-knit community among students within the discipline, while simultaneously connecting them with industry professionals. By providing these networking opportunities, we offer students a broader perspective on the engineering landscape, helping them navigate their future career paths. From academic support to social connection, we are committed to seeing our members thrive throughout their time at UC.
